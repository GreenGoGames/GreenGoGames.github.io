Privacy Policy

Data Controller

The data controller for GreenGoGames apps and games is GreenGoGames.

Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how GreenGoGames may collect and process data in connection with mobile applications and games that include advertising and/or analytics services.

Important: Some data types listed below (such as Apple IDFA / Google AAID) apply to mobile devices. If a specific product does not include advertising or analytics SDKs, those data types may not apply to that product.

Why does GreenGoGames collect data from its apps and games?

When you install and use GreenGoGames mobile applications, we can collect and process some of your data for targeting ads purposes.

You will find below explanations regarding the reasons why GreenGoGames may collect data and the legal bases GreenGoGames relies on in each case.

Purposes

  • To serve advertising tailored to the preference and interests of our users
  • To allow GreenGoGames to continue to provide free services and products

Legal basis

Express consent. In the countries where collecting users’ consent may be required by applicable laws and regulations, GreenGoGames only processes or shares personal data collected through GreenGoGames apps for personalized advertising purposes once you express your consent through the pop-up notice included in one of our apps.

What data does GreenGoGames collect from its apps and games?

For all the purposes listed above, the data that GreenGoGames collects is limited to:

  • Your Mobile Advertising ID (Apple IDFA or Google AAID) — technical identifiers developed by mobile operating systems for advertising purposes. These remain under your control and can be reset or erased at any time through your device settings.
  • Usage and interaction data pertaining to your activities on our applications, notably how and when you use our applications.
  • Advertising interaction data relating to advertising served in our applications (for example, number of ads served and potential clicks).

With whom may your data be shared & why?

GreenGoGames does not share your personal data with third parties without your prior consent in the countries where collecting users’ consent may be required by applicable laws and regulations.
